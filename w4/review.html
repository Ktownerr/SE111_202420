REVIEW

Keagan Towner January 29,2024

I would like to start off by stating the controversial FACT thatZack Snyder's Dawn of the Dead is much better (aside from the ending) than George Romero's. I personally believe Dawn of the Dead (2004) to be a close to perfect zombie film for a few reasons, the set design, the costuming , the camera shots, and most importantly the COLOR PALETTE are all so incredibly well done.

Trivia

  • Some of the camera crew would wear plastic sheeting during the filming of some of the gorier scenes, due to the amount of fake blood flying around.
  • Dawn of the Dead was shot in chronilogical order
  • The zombie extras were paid $8.50 an hour.
